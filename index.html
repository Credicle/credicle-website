Enabling under collatralized lending in Defi

We believe that the future of finance is governed by code. We build trust and transparency between a lender and their borrower, taking Defi mainstream and helping users make the transition from Web2 to Web3 confidently.

Increased data

Lending providers should have access to as much information about their borrower as possible

Credicle allows lenders to asses risk better, offering lower levels of collateral to consumers. Creating a lending environment that moves away from circular use cases of speculation and arbitrage.

Transparent and Holistic

Credicle combines on chain and off chain data, such as TradFi scores and KYC information, for a overall view of the borrower.

The Web3 score is minted into a soulbound NFT that is unique and sticky to a user + wallet combination.
A borrower can present their NFT to lending providers to validate their credit worthiness.
