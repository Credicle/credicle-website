About Credicle

Credicle is powering the transition of Defi through Web3 credit scores enabling under collateralized and fixed rate lending. We use on chain and off chain data to compute a credit score allowing lending protocols to asses risk better. Credicle is built by 2 Columbia University roomates studying CS-Econ and
CS-Math and is currently raising its seed round.
