Experience Credicle in Action

Our product is designed to revolutionize the way FinTech companies connect with their customers. With Credicle, lenders can assess risk better and offer lower levels of collateral to consumers. Our platform combines on chain and off chain data for a holistic view of the borrower, and generates a unique Web3 credit score that is minted into a soulbound NFT.

Key Features:

  • Increased access to data for better risk assessment
  • Lower collateral requirements for consumers
  • Transparent and holistic credit scoring
  • Unique Web3 credit score stored as an NFT
